Physics Department

Department Information :

The department of Physics was established in 1950.
This department deals with curriculum in Engineering Physics and Applied Physics common to all branches of first year engineering and polytechnic


1. Besides teaching activity the staff members of this department are involved in teaching of some materials like alloy, cement, lime, alum, bleaching powder, water and aggregates.
2. Also coordinate first year admission process & other activities of the collage.
3. In the last five years the faculty members of the department have contributed more than 30 research papers in international journals of repute and international conference proceedings.
